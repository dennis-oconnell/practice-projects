About Perseid Watch

The Perseid meteor shower is active every year from mid-July to late August. The next Perseids display will peak around the night of August 12 and before dawn on August 13, 2024.

Prepare to be awed by the spectacular Perseids meteor shower, one of the most anticipated celestial events of the year! Use Perseid Watch to track the date of the Perseids meteor shower, so you can watch as it grace the night sky with its mesmerizing display of shooting stars.

For more information check out https://www.timeanddate.com/astronomy/meteor-shower/perseid.html
